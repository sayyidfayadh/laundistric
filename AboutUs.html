About Us

  1. Home
  2. About Us

Laundristic LLC, a proud division of Al Otaiba Enterprises, is the UAE’s premier provider of professional garment care solutions. Since 2011, we’ve grown to serve all seven Emirates – delivering unparalleled quality, reliability, and innovation in every wash.

Guided by over 40 years of hands-on expertise from General Manager Abdul Azeez Ali, and under the visionary leadership of our Chairman and Founder, Mr. Otaiba Saeed Al Otaiba, Laundristic has become a trusted name across hospitality, aviation, corporate, and retail sectors.

From delicate couture to industrial volumes, our advanced systems, eco-conscious practices, and deep industry knowledge ensure consistent, premium care. Every item that enters our facility reflects a promise: cleaned to perfection, returned with pride.

Our Service Commitment

Available 24 Hours a Day, 7 Days a Week Same-Day Delivery Guarantee: Orders placed before 2:00 PM will be cleaned and delivered the same day Servicing wet cleaning, dry cleaning, laundry, uniform care, delicate garments, and more Fast pickup, meticulous processing, and on-time return – every time Exclusive Couture Cleaning: Alessia Couture We are proud to be the only couture cleaning service under Alessia Couture, personally managed by Mr. Abdul Azeez Ali. This elite service is designed for luxury fashion, designer gowns, and heirloom textiles, demanding absolute precision and discretion. Our process is both an art and a science—preserving the beauty, texture, and integrity of every exquisite piece. Laundristic doesn’t just clean garments – we restore elegance, protect value, and safeguard the trust of UAE’s most discerning clientele.

Meet Our Leadership

Mr. Otaiba Saeed Al Otaiba Chairman

Founder of Al Otaiba Enterprises, Mr. Otaiba is a distinguished business leader with a deep commitment to advancing key economic sectors in the UAE. His strategic vision and passion for quality laid the foundation for Laundristic’s rise as a high-end, technology-driven laundry service.

Mr. Abdul Azeez Ali
General Manager

With more than 40 years of global experience across the UAE, Europe, and the USA, Mr. Azeez brings unmatched expertise in both high-volume and premium garment care. From hotels and hospitals to offshore oilfields and couture boutiques, he continues to lead with operational brilliance and client-centric values.

Mr. Mahaboob Subhan Operations Manager

Trained under Mr. Azeez, Mr. Mahaboob Subhan brings extensive experience in administration, HR, operations, and quality control. Ensures effective team coordination, streamlined systems, and timely, professional service driving operational excellence and growth.

Mr. Narendra Puntambekar Laundry Manager

With an impressive background in hospitality and laundry operations, Mr. Narendra ensures exceptional service efficiency and smooth daily execution across all facilities. His unwavering dedication to client satisfaction and seamless logistics coordination plays a key role in our success.
